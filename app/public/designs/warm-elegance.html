Our Dance Disciplines

The Art of Latin Dance

Two traditions, one shared passion. Whether you're drawn to the electric precision of Salsa or the soulful intimacy of Bachata, we'll guide your journey from first step to social floor confidence.

01

Salsa On1 (LA Style)

Dynamic, linear, and endlessly expressive. LA-style Salsa On1 is built on crisp timing, elegant turn patterns, and musicality that makes every measure count. Our structured curriculum takes you from fundamental footwork to advanced partnerwork and dazzling shines.

All Levels Welcome
02

Bachata

Grounded, sensual, and deeply connected to the music. From Dominican roots to modern sensual styles, our Bachata program develops your body movement, lead-follow connection, and musical interpretation. Learn to tell stories through dance.

Beginner to Advanced

Meet Our Instructors

Every instructor at Salsa Ninja is a seasoned performer and dedicated teacher who brings passion, patience, and expertise to every class.

A

Sensei Alex

Head Instructor

Founder of Salsa Ninja with over 12 years of Salsa On1 experience. Trained under top LA-style masters, Alex is known for making complex patterns feel natural and achievable for every student.

M

Sensei Maria

Bachata Lead

Bachata specialist with roots in Dominican and modern sensual styles. Maria's classes are celebrated for deep musicality training and body movement drills that transform how students feel the music.

C

Sensei Carlos

Performance Director

Choreographer and competition team coach with multiple regional titles. Carlos brings stage presence, showmanship, and discipline to every performance routine he crafts.
