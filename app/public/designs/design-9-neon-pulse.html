// WHAT WE TEACH

MASTER YOUR STYLE

From the explosive energy of Salsa On1 to the sensual rhythms of Bachata, find your groove with expert instruction at every level.

💃

Salsa On1

LA-style Salsa On1 is all about explosive energy, sharp turns, and dynamic partner work. Our structured curriculum takes you from basic steps to advanced shines and complex combinations. Perfect for social dancing and performance.

Beginner Intermediate Advanced
🎶

Bachata

Bachata blends Dominican roots with modern sensual styling. Learn body movement, isolations, musicality, and partner connection. Our classes cover traditional, modern, and sensual Bachata styles for a complete experience.
