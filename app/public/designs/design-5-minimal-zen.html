What We Teach

We believe dance is a practice — a discipline that rewards patience, presence, and dedication. Every class is designed to build your confidence on the social floor and sharpen your artistry on stage.

Salsa On1 — LA Style

Dynamic, linear footwork with sharp turns and expressive body movement. Our Salsa program takes you from foundational timing and basic steps through intricate partner combinations and advanced styling.

Bachata

Sensual, grounded movement rooted in Dominican tradition. Learn authentic footwork, body rolls, and musicality. From traditional bachata to modern sensual fusion, develop your own voice on the dance floor.
