What We Teach

Two Styles, Endless Expression

From the explosive energy of Salsa On1 to the sensual flow of Bachata, discover the style that moves you.

💃

Salsa On1 (LA Style)

LA-style Salsa On1 is all about explosive energy, sharp turns, and dynamic partner work. Our structured curriculum takes you from basic steps to advanced shines and complex combinations. Perfect for social dancing and stage performance.

Beginner Intermediate Advanced
🎶

Bachata

Bachata blends Dominican roots with modern sensual styling. Learn body movement, isolations, musicality, and partner connection. Our classes cover traditional, modern, and sensual Bachata for a complete experience.
