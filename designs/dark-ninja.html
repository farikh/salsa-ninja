What We Teach

MASTER THE ART OF
LATIN DANCE

From your first step to competition-level performance, we train dancers who own the floor. Two core disciplines. One relentless standard of excellence.

🔥

SALSA ON1 (LA STYLE)

Fast, flashy, and precise. LA-style Salsa On1 is built for dancers who want to command attention. Linear patterns, explosive turn combinations, and musicality that makes every beat count. From beginner fundamentals to advanced shines and partnering.

All Levels Welcome
🎵

BACHATA

Sensual, grounded, and deeply musical. Our Bachata program covers Dominican roots through modern sensual styles. Learn to connect with your partner, interpret the music, and move with confidence at any social. Technique meets feeling.

Beginner to Advanced

Meet Your Senseis

WORLD-CLASS INSTRUCTORS

Every instructor at Salsa Ninja is a seasoned performer and dedicated teacher who lives and breathes Latin dance.

A

SENSEI ALEX

Head Instructor

Founder of Salsa Ninja with over 12 years of Salsa On1 experience. Trained under top LA-style masters. Known for breaking down complex patterns into clear, repeatable steps that stick.

M

SENSEI MARIA

Bachata Lead

Bachata specialist with roots in Dominican and sensual styles. Maria's classes are known for deep musicality training and body movement drills that transform how you feel the music.

C

SENSEI CARLOS

Performance Director

Choreographer and competition team coach. Carlos trains our performance squads and brings stage presence, showmanship, and team discipline to every routine. Multiple regional titles under his belt.
