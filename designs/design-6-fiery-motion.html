What We Teach

Find Your Rhythm

We believe dance is an art form that transforms your body, mind, and spirit. Our instructors combine technical precision with soulful expression to help you become the dancer you were meant to be.

LA Style

Salsa On1

Dynamic, linear footwork with sharp turns and explosive body movement. Our Salsa program takes you from foundational timing through intricate partner combinations and advanced styling that will make you unstoppable on the social floor.

Dominican Roots

Bachata

Sensual, grounded movement rooted in Dominican tradition. Learn authentic footwork, body rolls, and deep musicality. From traditional bachata to modern sensual fusion, develop your own voice and connection on the dance floor.
